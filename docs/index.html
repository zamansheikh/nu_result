About NU Result

NU Result is a completely static mobile application designed specifically for National University (Bangladesh) students. Our app provides a 100% offline tool to calculate your CGPA and generate professional academic marksheets with embedded QR codes.

Privacy-First Design: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations happen locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
